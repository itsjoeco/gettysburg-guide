Battlefield Day
Saturday, April 25

Breakfast: Pancakes & sausage (patrols cook & clean)

⚔️ James Colangelo — SPL (coordinating both groups)
🚶 Group A: Bill Yank Trail — 10-Mile Hike
Distance: 10 miles Difficulty: Moderate — rolling terrain, some rocky sections Est. Time: 6–7 hours with stops Pace: ~1.5 mi/hr (includes rest stops every hour) Water: Carry 1+ quart. Refill at Visitor Center restrooms (~mile 5)
Wear: Class A uniform + blue troop fleece. Good hiking boots.
Bring: Day pack, water, trail snacks, rain gear, sunscreen
📍 Key Stops
  • Seminary Ridge — Confederate artillery position, Day 1 of battle
  • McPherson's Ridge — Where the battle began
  • Eternal Light Peace Memorial
  • Oak Ridge / Barlow Knoll
  • Culp's Hill — Union right flank
  • Spangler's Spring
  • The Wheatfield — Fierce Day 2 fighting
  • Devil's Den — Famous sniper position & boulder field
  • Little Round Top — Crucial Union defense, Chamberlain's charge
  • The Peach Orchard
  • Virginia Memorial / Pickett's Charge — The "High Water Mark" of the Confederacy
  • Gettysburg National Cemetery — Lincoln's Gettysburg Address site

Packing & Gear

Check items off as you pack. State saves on your device.
👕 Clothing & Uniform
  • Class A uniform
  • Blue troop fleece
  • Good walking shoes / hiking boots
  • Change of clothes (2 days)
  • Rain gear / poncho
  • Extra layers (moisture-wicking, not cotton)
🛌 Sleeping
  • Sleeping bag
  • Sleeping pad
  • Camp pillow
🎒 10 Essentials
  • Pocketknife / multitool
  • First-aid kit (bandages, antiseptic, moleskin)
  • Water bottle (1+ quart)
  • Flashlight or headlamp
  • Trail food / high-energy snacks
  • Fire starters (matches, lighter, ferro rod)
  • Sun protection (SPF 30+, sunglasses, hat)
  • Map and compass
  • Whistle (for signaling)
  • Toiletries / personal hygiene (soap, toothpaste, sanitizer)
🌱 Other
  • Day pack / backpack
  • Mess kit in mesh bag (plate, bowl, cup, fork, knife, spoon)
  • Personal medication (notify health chaperone)
  • Book / card game / activity
